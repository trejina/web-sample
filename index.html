Disney California Adventure Park (often referred to as California Adventure or DCA), which debuted in 2001, is home to some one-of-a-kind attractions and themed lands such as Cars Land, Pixar Pier and the newly opened Avengers Campus. Fan favorite rides include the Incredicoaster, Guardians of the Galaxy: Mission BREAKOUT! and Radiator Springs Racers. California Adventure also offers great events and entertainment such as the annual Food & Wine Festival and World of Color nighttime spectacula water projection show where Disney and Pixar stories come to life.
